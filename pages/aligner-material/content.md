## Hero Section
**Headline:** Orthero clear aligners are manufactured using biocompatible plastic materials that prioritize your safety and comfort throughout your orthodontic treatment.

## Material Details

### PET-G (Polyethylenterephthalat-Glycol Copolyester)
Our aligners use PET-G, a food-grade material known for its exceptional biocompatibility and durability.

### Biocompatible & Safe
At Orthero, your health and well-being are our top priorities. That's why our aligners are made with biocompatible plastic materials that are safe to use in your mouth. Our aligners have undergone rigorous testing to ensure they meet stringent quality and safety standards, providing you with peace of mind throughout your treatment journey.

### Food-Grade Quality
Orthero clear aligners are made from PET-G, a material commonly used in food packaging due to its high level of safety and suitability for contact with consumables. This food-grade quality ensures that there are no harmful substances or chemicals in our aligners, keeping your oral health in focus while delivering effective orthodontic results.

### 100% Biomaterials
We take pride in utilizing 100% biomaterials in the production of our aligners. Our commitment to using biocompatible materials means that you can trust Orthero to provide aligners that are not only effective but also gentle on your teeth and gums.

## 3 Layer Technology
Orthero clear aligners feature a **3 layer technology** that enhances their durability and effectiveness. This innovative design ensures that each aligner is strong enough to withstand the forces of orthodontic movement while maintaining a comfortable fit throughout your treatment.

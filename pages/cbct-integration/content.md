# CBCT Integration Page Content

## Hero Section
**Headline:** At Orthero, we prioritize precision and accuracy in every aspect of our orthodontic treatments. Our CBCT integration is a testament to this commitment, revolutionizing the way we approach tooth alignment and biomechanics.

## Features

### 1. Real Teeth Axis Data
Through the integration of CBCT technology, we eliminate all assumptions by utilizing real teeth axis data. This ensures that each aligner is precisely designed to match the unique shape and orientation of your teeth, leading to optimal tooth alignment and a seamless smile transformation.

### 2. Mandibular Rotation Simulation
The mandibular rotation, a crucial factor in orthodontic treatments, is simulated using the real hinge axis set from CBCT analysis. This advanced functionality allows us to take into account the unique movement and rotation of your jaw, enhancing the precision and effectiveness of your orthodontic treatment.

### 3. Optimized Biomechanics
Biomechanics play a vital role in the success of orthodontic treatment. With CBCT integration, we can position attachments perfectly according to the real center of resistance, optimizing the force distribution and ultimately leading to more efficient tooth movement and desired treatment outcomes.

## Benefits
- **No Assumptions** - Real data, not estimates
- **Precise Design** - Matched to unique tooth shape
- **Jaw Movement** - Accounts for mandibular rotation
- **Optimal Force** - Perfect attachment positioning
- **Better Outcomes** - More efficient tooth movement
